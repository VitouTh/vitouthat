Research Focusកិច្ចការស្រាវជ្រាវ
01

AI-Native Semantic Communication

Developing AI-native wireless communication systems that transmit semantic information rather than raw data. Applying Transformer-based shared embedding and attention mechanisms for semantic compression and recovery, and DRL to control resource allocation and scheduling.

02

AI‑Based Intelligent Resource Management

Designing resource control algorithms using Multi-Agent Deep Reinforcement Learning for power control, beamforming, and interference management. Learning‑based QoS assurance via Deep Learning to maintain latency, jitter, and service reliability in dynamic network environments.

03

AI for Space–Air–Ground Integrated Networks (SAGIN) & UAV Communications

Learning‑based optimization of 3D integrated networks spanning satellites, UAVs, and terrestrial systems. Joint optimization of UAV trajectories, altitudes, wireless power transfer, and beamforming using DRL with Federated Learning combined with evolutionary algorithms.
